SAS Developer/Programmer

Description

Our client would like to reinforce their team with a consultant, who will take care of day to day execution of data processes and assist in new developments (reportings and Commercial steering systems).


A Typical Week
  • Development and adjusting SAS Base & SAS MACRO code.
  • Functional documentation of existing and new data process solutions.
  • Running of weekly/monthly commercial reportings.
  • Data Management Activities & Data Validation.
  • Testing new developed solutions.

Requirements
  • Native French or Dutch (and B2 level in the other language) and B2 level of English minimum.
  • Very good knowledge of SAS BASE (Advanced coding).
  • Very good knowledge of both SAS MACRO/SAS SQL.
  • Very good knowledge of Microsoft Excel.

Bonus
  • Good knowledge of Microsoft Visio/Microsoft Access/Visual Basic/HTML5/CSS3.
  • SAS 9 certification.
  • Banking experience and/or Mathematics background.
Location

Brussels, Belgium.

Experience

7 years of experience minimum.

Salary Range

€510 - €545/day.

Client

International Banking client.
