Data Modeler

Description

Design and maintain the different data models of the Group’s BI/Data platform.


Typical Week
  • Banking industry modeling.
  • Build the conceptual model in collaboration with the different business stakeholders.
  • Build the data models of the new BI/Data platform: Integration/Enterprise layer and Data Marts.
  • Set up data modeling practice within the team: set up data modeling best practices from and outside the industry.
  • Share the knowledge within and outside the team pro actively.
  • Collaborate with Business, functional analysts and Solution Architect.
  • Understand business requirements and build a functional analysis which covers needs of the business according to the target BI reference architecture.

Requirements
  • Native French or Dutch and B2 level of English minimum.
  • Conceptual, Logical and Physical modeling.
  • Proven track with datawarehouse modeling approaches: DataVault, Kimball, Immon.
  • Experience with Enterprise data model is a must.
  • Experience in an agile delivery environment.
  • Experience with Data Modeling tools like: Enterprise Architect, Erwin, PowerDesigner.
  • Good Communication and influencing skills.
  • Good analytical and synthesis skills.

Bonus
  • Experience in Private/investment banking is a plus.
Location

Brussels, Belgium.

Experience

7 years of experience minimum.

Salary Range

€550 - €585/day.

Client

Belgian Banking client.
